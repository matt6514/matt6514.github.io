Want to get SCUBA certified?

SCUBA diving is the primary method for exploring and searching ship wrecks. It cant be extremely dangerous and should not be attempted without a license and proper safety precautions in place. To learn more about how to get SCUBA certified in your area and how to stay safe while exploring ship wreck check out our page on SCUBA diving
